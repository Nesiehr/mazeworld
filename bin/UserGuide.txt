Welcome to MazeWorld!
Goal: Solve the maze by reaching the purple square
Key events:
  n - creates a new maze
  v - creates a new maze with predominantly vertical lines
  h - creates a new maze with predominantly horizontal lines
  r - resets the current maze
  d - solves the maze by depth-first search
  b - solves the maze by breadth-first search
  s - toggles showing the user path
  space - toggle heatmap view
  arrow keys - move around the maze
